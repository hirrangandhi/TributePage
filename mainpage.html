Vijay

Thalapathy Vijay in Mersal movie


Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar (born 22 June 1974), known professionally as Vijay, is an Indian actor, dancer, playback singer and Philanthropist who works in Tamil cinema. He's referred to by fans and media as 'Ilaya Thalapathy' and also as 'Thalapathy'.

At 10 years old, Vijay's first role was in the drama Vetri (1984); he continued to perform as a child actor in some films. Vijay made his debut as an main lead in "Nalaiya Theerpu(1992)" which was directed by his father S.A.Chandrasekhar. But, people criticized him for his look in the movie. Vijay doesn't lose his hope; he tried to do his best and continued to act in movies.

Then,In 1996, Vijay performed in the Vikraman-directed "Poove Unakkaga", which became his first acclaimed performance as well as the breakthrough in Vijay's career and made him a recognizable star. In 1997, "Kadhalukku Mariyadhai", directed by Fazil, earned Vijay a Tamil Nadu State Film Award for Best Actor. In 1999, Vijay acted in debutant Ezhil's "Thulladha Manamum Thullum", with Simran, which ran for more than 200 days at the Tamil Nadu box office and earned Vijay a Tamil Nadu State Film Award for Best Film.

In late 2003, Vijay acted in "Thirumalai" directed by Ramana, which featured him in a different way and it made him as a pakka commercial mass actor. He started following the same template commercial pattern and acted "Ghilli" which was a mega blockbuster. Ghilli broke all the previous records in the Kollywood industry. 2006-2010 can be easily said as the worst phase of Vijay's career. He faced continuous flops in the period.

Then, In 2012, he gave comeback in "Thuppaki" directed by A.R. Murugadoss, which collected 100 crore in the box-office. From 2012 to till now, Vijay's growth in Kollywood industry is in top gear. His movies has done well in box-office and received well by people. In between 2011-2019, he made some remarkable movies like Nanban,Thalaivaa, Kaththi, Puli, Theri, Mersal and Sarkar and Bigil. Recently, he acted "Master" along with Vijay Sethupathi, directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj which is awaiting for release.

Vijay is also a Playback singer and sang nearly 30+ songs.
